RULES

BOYS:

  • Team events to be conducted.
  • Each team should have a minimum of 4 players in the team and maximum of 7 players in the team
  • Teams having less than 4 players or more than 7 players will be disqualified.
  • Each team game will be best of five. The sequence of a team game is as follows
    • 1st match: 1st Singles
    • 2nd match: 1st Doubles
    • 3rd match: 2nd Singles
    • 4th match: 2nd Doubles (if required)
    • 5th match: 3rd Singles (if required)
  • Each match will be best of three games, 21 points each.
  • Against a team, a player cannot play more than 2 matches.
  • All matches will be played as per the rules of the Badminton Association of India (BAI).
  • Referee decisions will be final and the organizing committee hold the rights to disallow a player from playing further.

GIRLS:

  • Team events to be conducted.
  • At least 2 players are required and maximum of 5 players are allowed.
  • Teams having less than 2 players or more than 5 players will be disqualified.
  • The sequence of a team game is as follows:
  • Each match is best of three games, 21 points each.
    • 1st match: 1st Singles
    • 2nd match: 1st Doubles
    • 3rd match : 2nd Singles (If required)
  • Against a team, only two players from the team can play (at max) two matches (not both singles) and no player can play more than 2 matches.
  • All matches will be played as per the rules of the Badminton Association of India (BAI).
  • Referee decisions will be final and the organizing committee hold the rights to disallow a player from playing further.

Fees(Men): Rs. 2200
